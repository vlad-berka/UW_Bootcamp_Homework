BIO

After graduating from Western Washington University, friends Harrison Mills & Clayton Knight decided to merge into the DJ duo known as ODESZA. They've kickstarted their musical stardom in 2012 with the iconic album Summer's Gone and followed it with In Return in 2014 to critical acclaim. Their growing success has gained them their first Grammy nomination for Say My Name in 2016, a second nomination for A Moment Apart in 2017, and a third nomination for Line of Sight in 2018.

After having strongly toured their "A Moment Apart" album for almost two years, the duo are laying low in Bellingham, WA while releasing music sproradically - much to the chagrin of many fans. They are currently actively collaborating with Golden Features under the name BRONSON, but have yet to release a new album themselves. I guess we just have to wait patiently and see!
